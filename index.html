Our Story

Founded in 2024, The Cozy Corner Café was born from a simple idea: to create a peaceful haven for our community. We believe in the power of a good cup of coffee to bring people together. All our beans are ethically sourced, and our pastries are baked fresh every morning with love. Come in, relax, and make yourself at home.
